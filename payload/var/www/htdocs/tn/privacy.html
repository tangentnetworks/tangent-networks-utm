Privacy Policy

Last Updated On: Sat Dec 6 11:14:50 AM IST 2025

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines the principles, commitments, and operational practices of Tangent Networks ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") with respect to the handling of information through the Tangent Networks WebUI Application ("Application"), built for managing Firewall, Routing, Intrusion Detection and Prevention Systems (IDPS), and associated network security mechanisms. This Policy is drafted to ensure transparency, safeguard user interests, and comply with the applicable legal framework within the jurisdiction of Kerala, India.

1. Scope of the Policy

This Policy governs the access, usage, and interaction with the Application by administrators, operators, or any authorized users ("Users"). It applies exclusively to the data handled within the Application and does not extend to any third-party systems, external integrations, or independent network components unless expressly incorporated by reference.

2. Foundational Declaration: No Unsolicited Data Collection

We hereby expressly affirm and warrant that:

(a) The Application does not collect, transmit, store, or process personal, behavioral, or operational data from Users unless the User voluntarily and explicitly submits such information.

(b) The Application does not embed analytics trackers, telemetry systems, profiling mechanisms, or passive surveillance technologies.

(c) Any data provided to us for troubleshooting, diagnostics, or technical assistance is furnished solely at the User's discretion and shall be handled strictly in accordance with this Policy.

3. Information We May Receive (Only If You Provide It)

The only categories of information we may possess are those voluntarily offered by Users for troubleshooting purposes, which may include:

(a) System configuration files (e.g., firewall rulesets, routing tables, IDPS signatures).

(b) Error logs, crash reports, or performance metrics as exported by the User.

(c) Screenshots or step-by-step descriptions supplied for issue reproduction.

(d) Contact details voluntarily submitted for support correspondence.

No other data is collected, inferred, mined, or aggregated.

4. Data Minimization Principle

Any information furnished voluntarily by the User shall be limited to the minimum required for addressing the specific concern raised. We do not request, encourage, or require broader access to system data beyond the narrow technical necessity communicated to the User beforehand.

5. Use of Information

Any voluntarily provided information shall be used solely for:

(a) Diagnosing, validating, and resolving technical issues.

(b) Enhancing compatibility or patching specific operational bugs encountered.

(c) Providing feedback or clarifications requested by the User.

We do not use User-provided information for marketing, research, analytics, profiling, or any other secondary purpose.

6. Storage, Retention, and Erasure

By engaging with support services, you agree to the following binding terms regarding the handling of information you voluntarily provide:

(a) Information shared voluntarily may be stored only for the duration necessary to resolve the issue.

(b) Upon completion of the support task, such data shall be securely deleted unless the User expressly authorizes continued retention for future reference.

(c) No backups or secondary copies shall be maintained without explicit written consent from the User.

(d) Users may request immediate deletion at any point during the support process.

7. Security of Voluntarily Shared Data

We adopt commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any voluntarily provided data, including:

(a) Controlled access restrictions.

(b) Encrypted communication channels when exchanging support files.

(c) Secure wiping procedures upon deletion.

(d) Internal confidentiality obligations binding upon our personnel and contractors.

8. No Integration With Third-Party Data Processors

We do not outsource, transmit, or transfer User-provided information to any third-party service provider, cloud platform, or analytics tool unless explicitly requested by the User for specialized debugging support.

9. Cookies, Telemetry, and Automated Tracking

The Application does not use cookies, browser-based tracking, device fingerprinting, or embedded telemetry frameworks. The WebUI operates entirely on-device, within the User's infrastructure, without remote monitoring or automated reporting.

10. Network Traffic and System Logs

All operational logs (firewall events, routing logs, IDPS alerts, system-level diagnostics) are generated and stored locally within the User's infrastructure. We have no access to such logs unless the User exports and shares them manually.

11. User Rights

By voluntarily submitting information, you retain the following express rights regarding its use and management:

(a) Right to know precisely how the information will be used.

(b) Right to withhold or refuse submission of any data.

(c) Right to request deletion at any time.

(d) Right to request clarification, correction, or redaction of shared materials.

(e) Right to obtain confirmation that no other data about them exists in our systems.

12. Children's Privacy

The Application is a professional network administration tool and is not designed, marketed, or intended for use by children or minors. We do not knowingly collect any information from individuals below 18 years of age.

13. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update or revise this Policy to reflect legal, technological, or operational changes. All modifications shall apply prospectively and shall be made accessible to Users through official channels. Continued use of the Application after such updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

14. Legal Compliance and Jurisdiction

This Policy is governed by, and shall be construed in accordance with, the laws applicable in the State of Kerala, Republic of India. Any disputes arising out of or related to this Policy shall be subject exclusively to the jurisdiction of the competent courts situated in Kerala, India.

15. Contact Information

For requests, clarifications, deletions, or legal notices regarding this Policy, Users may contact Tangent Networks through the designated communication channels as provided in the Application or associated documentation.

16. Final Declaration

By using the Tangent Networks WebUI Application, Users acknowledge that the Application itself performs no data collection and that any information received by us is voluntarily supplied by the User for troubleshooting or support purposes only.
